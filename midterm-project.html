1. Project Overview

In this project, you will analyze a real-world systems problem and produce a formal Systems Architecture and Systems Integration document. The focus is not on building the system, but on thinking like a systems architect: defining boundaries, identifying interacting subsystems, modeling business processes, and justifying architectural decisions.

2. Project Objectives

  • Analyze real-world problems from a systems perspective.
  • Design a high-level system architecture and model business processes.
  • Identify and justify integration mechanisms.
  • Communicate design decisions using standard diagrams.

3. Project Scope & Constraints

‚úÖ Required Characteristics

  • Multiple interacting subsystems
  • At least one external system/service
  • Clear end-to-end business process
  • Data exchange or integration required

üö´ Not Allowed

  • Single-user or single-component systems
