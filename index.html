Course Schedule

Detailed breakdown of lectures, exams, and holidays.

Week 1: Intro to Systems Integration (SIA) Jan 8
Sub-Topics:
  • Scope of SIA and key concepts
  • Systems thinking approach
Outcome: Explain scope, purpose, and key concepts of SIA.
Week 2: Role of the Systems Architect Jan 15
Sub-Topics:
  • Responsibilities & Skills
  • Decision-making in architecture
Outcome: Describe the central role and tasks of a systems architect for modeling & integration.
Week 3: Monolithic vs Microservices Jan 22
Sub-Topics:
  • Characteristics of Monoliths
  • Benefits & Trade-offs of Microservices
Outcome: Differentiate monolithic vs microservices and understand model implications.
Week 4: System vs. Software Architecture Jan 29
Sub-Topics:
  • Architecture views & model boundaries
  • C4 Model vs UML
Outcome: Distinguish system vs software architecture and apply modeling views.
Week 5: Business Process Modeling (BPMN) Feb 5
Sub-Topics:
  • BPMN elements & Process orchestration
  • Integration touchpoints
Outcome: Create BPMN diagrams representing organizational processes.
Week 6: UML for System Modeling Feb 12
Sub-Topics:
  • Use Case Diagrams
  • Class & Sequence Diagrams
Outcome: Create UML diagrams for system requirements and interactions.
Week 7: Integration Flows (UML + BPMN) Feb 19
Sub-Topics:
  • Integration patterns
  • Orchestration logic
Outcome: Produce UML/BPMN diagrams depicting integration flows.
Week 8: SysML for Systems Architecture Feb 26
Sub-Topics:
  • Block definition diagrams
  • Internal block diagrams & parametrics
Outcome: Create SysML diagrams representing high-level system architecture.
Week 9: Midterm Examination Mar 5
Assessment Focus:

Model interpretation and critique.

Outcome: Analyze and critique system models; Assess understanding of architectural concepts.
Week 10: Modeling Integration Patterns Mar 12
Sub-Topics:
  • Layered Architecture
  • SOA & Event-driven diagrams
Outcome: Model common integration patterns using UML/SysML.
Week 11: API and Service Modeling Mar 19
Sub-Topics:
  • Service interfaces & interactions
  • Sequence, Component, and Deployment diagrams
Outcome: Produce diagrams showing API interaction and deployment flows.
Week 12: Data Modeling for Integrated Systems Mar 26
Sub-Topics:
  • Logical data models
  • Message schemas & Canonical models
Outcome: Create models representing data structures and flow.
University Days / Holy Week Mar 27 - Apr 3

Sub-Topics:
  • Threat modeling & Access flows
  • Trust zones
Outcome: Depict security boundaries and trust zones in system models.
Week 14: Documentation & Governance Apr 16
Sub-Topics:
  • Viewpoints & Traceability
  • Versioning & Ownership
Outcome: Document models with traceability and governance principles.
Week 15: Legacy Systems & Modernization Apr 23
Sub-Topics:
  • As-is vs To-be models
  • Refactoring & modernization strategies
Outcome: Create integrated models for modernization and end-to-end system analysis.

Outcome: Students present and defend their integrated system models.
